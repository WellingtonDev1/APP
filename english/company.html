history

After the bankruptcy of a programmers company located in the city of SP, a huge number of employees ended up losing their jobs, after the dissolution, a group of 6 young people got together after the last day of work in a room, after a conversation long, seeing that they both worked in different areas and had intimacy within the already bankrupt company, these 6 young people decided to get together to start their own software company, the name APP came from the exact 3 points that the young people considered decadent in his old company, agility without losing quality, care with processes and love for programming.

Mission

APP offers the best and most advanced options in software for corporate management, always seeking to update itself on the best in the market, bringing more and more people to the world of technology, adding great experience to those who constitute it, as well as , valuing quality over quantity.

Vision

Reach 20% of the technology and development sector in the foreign market within 2 years and increase our visibility by 40% in the domestic market over the next year.

Values

  • Transparency in Processes
  • Commitment to Completion of Projects
  • Agility without Loss of Quality
  • Lasting Partnerships
  • honesty and empathy
  • Motivating partnership between managers and employees
